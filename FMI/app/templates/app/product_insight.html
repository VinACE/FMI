Fragrentica

Analyze the Product Intelligence sites.

Analyze the Product Intelligence sites. The Fragrantica site holds product information on almost all Fragrances. Each fragrance is reviewed the fragrantica team on its characteristics. Next the public can also add reviews for a fragrance.
The following facets are scraped from each fragrance: fragrance, accords, moods, notes, review-date, review, sentiment-label (pos, neg, neutral). Note that a label is added to each review based on a sentiment analysis using NLTK toolkit.
